What is process serving?
We deliver your legal documents
When you take someone to court, they legally need to be told about it. That's called "being served." YBS sends a certified server to hand your documents directly to them — then gives you an official signed Affidavit of Service for the court.
✅
What you get
Live GPS tracking · Signed affidavit · Auto court filing · Done in hours
⚠️
Important
YBS delivers documents — we don't give legal advice. If you need legal help, visit Legal Aid Ontario or your local community legal clinic.
